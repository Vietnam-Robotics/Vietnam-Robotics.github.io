About the Day

Overview

The Symposium on Robotics & Intelligent Systems invites the broader robotics community to share and explore advances in explicit and implicit model-based control and their applications in modern robotics. The event also serves as a platform to foster stronger collaboration and innovation across academia and industry, research and development, and all layers of the robotics stack.

What to expect

  • Keynotes unpacking learning and control for different robot forms
  • Peer-reviewed lightning talks sourced from research labs and startups
  • Recent research and development results from academia and industry

Call for Contributions

Topics of Interest

Robot learning & adaptive control

Implicit and explicit model control approaches and their applications.

Loco-manipulation & whole-body control

Coordinated control of contact-rich behaviors, real-time control of high-DOF systems.

Aerial, manipulation & legged robotics

Hardware-aware motion planning, control, and cross-domain autonomy.
